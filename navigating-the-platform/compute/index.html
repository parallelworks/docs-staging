Compute

Spin up and manage your available compute resources using the Compute Resources Interface. The primary interface display shows your desired resource settings (min/max CPUs), the status of your requested cores, and the approximate volume of service units consumed by a given resource.

Select desired resource level Move blue sliders to select desired resource levels. The sliders represent the maximum and minimum number of workers for a given cluster.

Power up Click the power button to activate resources and spin up cluster. The green bar and circle represent the active number of workers.

Resource Monitor

Once you have activated your resources, click the blue "i" icon to view the status of your compute resources as Parallel.Works spins up your cluster. See requested and active workers as well as the job staging for your workflow tasks.
